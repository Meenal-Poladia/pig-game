Instructions To Play The Dice Game



  • 1. Pig is a simple dice game first described in print by John Scarne in 1945.

  • 2. Players take turns to roll a single die as many times as they wish, adding all roll results to a running total, but losing their gained score for the turn if they roll a 1

  • 3. Player can add their gained scores and swicthing the game to the next player by pressing the hold button

  • 4. Player wins if the score crosses 50.
